About This App

MyFacial.App is purely an application that created in order to detect every human expression in the world. This app can classify up to 7 expression: Anger, Happy, Sad, Fear, Disgust, Neutral, and Surprise.

MyFacial.App also support privacy issue, therefore, we would not use your face as anything as only displaying the result to you. You can also capture the result and keep it for yourself without worry.

The reason behind the creation of MyFacial.App is for the research purposes only.
